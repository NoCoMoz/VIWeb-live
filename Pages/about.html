Our Mission

Voices Ignited is a multi-faceted organization created by the people, for the people. We believe that every person deserves and holds the inherent right to food, shelter, medical care, and education. We believe it is the duty of our government to ensure basic needs of all citizens are not only met, but prioritized. We deserve and demand a government that works for OUR best interests, and puts the welfare and prosperity of its citizens above all else. Under this cause, we the people have the right to use our voices, granted to us under the Constitution that unites us as one.

Pillars of Action:

Education and Resources:

Voices Ignited will design a network of educational resources that allow everyone to expand and discuss their knowledge without fear of censorship and supression.

Community Building and Mutual Aid:

Voices Ignited will build communities across the nation to facilitate and promote mutual aid efforts.

Demonstrations and Events:

Voices Ignited will organize and faciliate demonstrations across the nation that will combat govenrment anuse of power and opression of the American people.

Independant Media Production and Journalism:

Voices Ignited will create independent media through various mediums and platforms that reflect the values and opinions of the people.

Uplifting Mraginalized Groups and Communities:

Voices Ignited stands for intersectionality and equality, no expections. We cannot be free unless all of us are free.

Politics and Elections:

Voices Ignited is not about left vs. right, we are about up vs. down; the oligarchy vs. the people. We believe the United States requires a government uncorrupted by greed, and therefore, will work to institute working class Americans into elected positions.
